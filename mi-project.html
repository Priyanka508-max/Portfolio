Mi(Xiaomi) E-commerce Website Clone

The MI Website Clone Project aims to replicate the design and functionality of the official Mi Xiaomi Website website using HTML, CSS, jQuery, and JavaScript. Homepage Clone: Recreated the layout and design of the Mi official website homepage, including the header, navigation menu, hero section, featured content, and footer. Interactive Elements: Enhanced user engagement with interactive elements such as hover effects, clickable buttons, animated transitions. Product/Service Pages: Clone the layout and design of specific product or service pages from the Mi website, incorporating detailed information, images, videos, and specifications. Contact Form: Created a contact form with input fields for name, email, subject, and message, allowing visitors to reach out to the company easily. Social Media Integration: Integrated social media icons and sharing buttons to encourage visitors to connect with www.resumod.co Created with Mi group on various platforms and share content. Footer: Replicated the footer section with links to important pages, legal information, privacy policy, and copyright notice, maintaining consistency with the original website.
